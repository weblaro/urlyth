Cookie Policy

Urlyth does not use tracking or advertising cookies. However, we use "local storage" technologies to ensure the extension functions correctly.

1. Local Storage

We use browser localStorage and chrome.storage to save your notes, memos, and settings. This data stays on your device and is not shared with us.

2. Essential Session Data

Our payment provider, ExtensionPay (Stripe), may use essential cookies to manage your subscription status and prevent fraud during the checkout process.

3. Managing Data

You can clear your data at any time using the "Nuclear Delete" or "Clear" buttons within the Urlyth side panel settings.
